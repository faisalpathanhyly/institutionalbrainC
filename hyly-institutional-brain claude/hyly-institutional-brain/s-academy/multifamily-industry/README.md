# Multifamily Industry

> Institutional knowledge covering the multifamily real estate industry, its structure, key players, terminology, and market dynamics.

## What This Domain Covers

This domain captures foundational and advanced knowledge about the multifamily housing industry — the primary market Hyly operates in. It serves as the industry knowledge base for onboarding, training, client conversations, and AI-assisted learning.

## Why It Matters

Hyly's products and services are purpose-built for multifamily operators. Deep industry fluency enables the team to serve clients more effectively, build better products, and position Hyly credibly in the market.
