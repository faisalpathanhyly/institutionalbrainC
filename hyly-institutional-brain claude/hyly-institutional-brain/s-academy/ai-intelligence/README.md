# AI Intelligence

> Knowledge about AI systems, tools, capabilities, and how Hyly applies AI across its products and operations.

## What This Domain Covers

This domain captures the organization's growing body of knowledge around artificial intelligence — how AI works, how Hyly uses it, how it intersects with the multifamily industry, and how the team should think about AI-assisted workflows.

## Why It Matters

AI is central to Hyly's product direction and the Knowledge Management practice itself. Structured AI knowledge ensures the team stays aligned, builds on shared understanding, and applies AI capabilities consistently and thoughtfully.
