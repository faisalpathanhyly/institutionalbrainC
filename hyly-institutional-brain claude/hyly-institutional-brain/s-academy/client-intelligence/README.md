# Client Intelligence

> Structured knowledge about Hyly's clients — their profiles, needs, behaviors, and relationships.

## What This Domain Covers

This domain captures structured intelligence about Hyly's client base. It is not a CRM. It is an organized knowledge layer that helps the team understand clients at a deeper level — their business models, pain points, organizational structures, and how they use Hyly's products.

## Why It Matters

Client intelligence enables the entire organization — from Sales to CSM to Product — to operate with shared, accurate, up-to-date knowledge about the clients Hyly serves.

## Knowledge Areas

_To be populated as knowledge assets are added._

- Client profiles and segments
- Common client personas
- Client success patterns
- Client pain points by segment
- Onboarding and relationship patterns
- Renewal and expansion triggers
