# S.Academy

S.Academy is Hyly's AI-backed learning platform.

Its responsibility is to **transform institutional knowledge into structured learning experiences**. It consumes knowledge from the Institutional Brain — it does not own knowledge.

## What S.Academy Delivers

- Courses
- Assessments
- AI-guided tutoring
- Knowledge assets for learners

## Architecture Note

S.Academy is the consumer, not the source of truth. Knowledge lives in the Institutional Brain. S.Academy organizes and delivers that knowledge to learners through its domains.

As new delivery formats emerge, they consume the same knowledge — they do not recreate it.
