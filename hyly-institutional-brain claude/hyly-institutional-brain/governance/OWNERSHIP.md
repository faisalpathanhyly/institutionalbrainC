## Responsibilities

**Repository Owner** is responsible for:
- Overall architectural integrity
- Approving governance changes
- Resolving cross-domain conflicts
- Long-term direction

**Domain Stewards** are responsible for:
- Quality and accuracy of knowledge within their domain
- Reviewing contributions to their domain
- Maintaining `meta.json` for their domain
- Flagging outdated or conflicting knowledge
